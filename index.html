About us

Rise up Women is a Christian philanthropic organization that was founded to help and assist women who are experiencing physical, verbal and emotional abuse, temporal separation, those who are going through divorce, and those who are experiencing severe emotional difficulties.

This organization also aims to assist women who are either experiencing substance abuse or are recovering. We encourage women in such situations and bring the love of Christ into their issues to lift them up positively.
